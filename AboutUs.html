Welcome to CyberSpace Cafe!
At CyberSpace Cafe, we are dedicated to providing a top-notch computing and social experience for our customers. Founded in 2010, our cafe is a haven for tech enthusiasts, gamers, and anyone looking to connect in a comfortable and friendly environment.

Our Mission
Our mission is to offer a versatile space where technology and community converge. Whether you're here to browse the internet, complete a project, or enjoy the latest games, CyberSpace Cafe has the resources and atmosphere you need.

Our Story
CyberSpace Cafe began with a simple idea: to create a place where technology and community come together. Our founders,Mr. Nitin Kumar Nigam, envisioned a space that would cater to the diverse needs of our local community. From gamers seeking high-performance rigs to professionals needing a reliable workspace, our cafe has grown to meet the evolving demands of our customers.

What We Offer
State-of-the-Art Equipment: Our cafe is equipped with the latest high-performance computers, peripherals, and software to ensure a smooth and efficient user experience. From high-speed internet to cutting-edge gaming rigs, we have it all.

Comfort and Convenience:
Enjoy a relaxed ambiance with comfortable seating, ambient lighting, and a variety of refreshments. Our cafe provides a welcoming atmosphere where you can unwind, socialize, or focus on your work.

Expert Support:
Our knowledgeable staff is always on hand to assist with any technical issues or questions. Whether you need help with a specific task or general tech advice, we are here to support you.

Our Services
  • Internet Access: Fast and reliable internet connections for browsing, streaming, and more.
  • Gaming Zone: A dedicated area for gamers with high-end PCs, the latest games, and peripherals.
  • Workspace: Quiet and well-equipped workstations for students, freelancers, and professionals.
  • Printing and Scanning: Convenient printing, scanning, and copying services.
  • Events and Workshops: Regular events, gaming tournaments, and tech workshops to bring the community together.
Our Commitment
At CyberSpace Cafe, we believe in fostering a community where technology serves as a bridge to connect people. We are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and innovative space for all our visitors.
